Davante Adams

Davante Lavell Adams

Position: WR

6-1, 215lb (185cm, 97kg)

Team: Las Vegas Raiders

Born: December 24, 1992 in Redwood City, CA

College: Fresno St.  (College Stats)

Weighted Career AV (100-95-...): 84 (354th overall since 1960)

High School: Palo Alto (CA)

Draft: Green Bay Packers in the 2nd round (53rd overall) of the 2014 NFL Draft.

Pronunciation: \duh-VAWN-tay\
